I am **Youngjin Kim**, and this blog is where I keep technical notes that I want to revisit later without digging through bookmarks or private drafts.

The writing here is intentionally practical:

- algorithm problem solving
- backend and database notes
- web platform details that are easy to forget
- change logs when I replace old tools with simpler ones

I prefer short explanations, examples that actually run, and posts that show what changed my mind during the work.

Some posts are in English, some include Korean, and most of them are written as working notes rather than polished essays.

You can find the code and the rest of my public work on [GitHub](https://github.com/Youngjin-KimY).
